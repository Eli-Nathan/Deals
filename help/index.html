Step 1

Go to the Affinity App and login.

Step 2

Choose the 4 deals you want. (Screenshot below)

Step 3

Enter the info into the app. (Screenshot below)

Step 4

To get the the image path: Right click the image of the car and choose Inspect. Then you should see the URL of the background image. Right click the blue URL and select: Open in new tab. (Screenshots below)

Step 5

Copy the URL. You can then paste this into the Image URL field in the App (Screenshot below)

Step 6

Rinse and repeat for all 4 deals. You can then click Generate JSON to download a JSON file for use on the website and Generate CSV to download a csv for use in print.
